About the Game

Arcade Runner is an offline, lightweight, addictive mobile game designed for quick sessions and endless fun. Jump, dodge, and survive as long as you can while collecting coins. You can also log in with your Gmail account to save your high score and track your progress!

✈️ Play completely offline anywhere

Why You'll Love It

Whether you have 2 minutes or 20, Arcade Runner delivers instant fun. Built with performance and simplicity in mind, it runs smoothly on most devices and keeps you coming back for "just one more run".
