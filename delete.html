Delete Your Arcade Runner Account

Learn how to permanently delete your account and associated data.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

🧭 How to Delete Your Account

  • Open the Arcade Runner app
  • Navigate to Profile or Settings
  • Tap on "Delete Account"
  • Confirm deletion

🗑️ What Data Gets Deleted

  • Name and profile information
  • Email address (Google Sign-In via Firebase Authentication)
  • App usage data associated with the account

⏳ Data Retention Policy

  • Some system logs may be retained for up to 30 days for security and fraud prevention
  • No personally identifiable data is stored after deletion beyond this period
